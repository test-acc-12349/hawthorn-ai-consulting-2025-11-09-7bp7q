n8n is an open-source, node-based workflow automation platform that stands out for its flexibility and power. Unlike traditional RPA tools, n8n offers:

  • • Visual Workflow Builder: Intuitive drag-and-drop interface that doesn't require coding
  • • 500+ Integrations: Connect virtually any application or service
  • • Self-Hosted Option: Deploy on your own infrastructure for maximum security
  • • Cost-Effective: Significantly lower pricing compared to enterprise RPA tools

Hawthorn AI specializes in leveraging n8n's capabilities to create custom automation solutions tailored to your specific business needs.

Implementation timelines vary based on complexity and scope, but here's what to expect:

  • • Simple Workflows: 1-2 weeks (single system integration)
  • • Moderate Workflows: 2-4 weeks (3-5 system integrations)
  • • Complex Enterprise Solutions: 4-12 weeks (multiple departments, custom logic)

Most clients see their first workflows live and generating ROI within 2-4 weeks. We follow an agile approach, delivering incremental value while building toward your complete automation roadmap.

  • Compliance: GDPR, SOC2, and enterprise compliance ready

Our team follows industry best practices and conducts security audits on all implementations. We work with your IT department to ensure compliance with your security policies.
